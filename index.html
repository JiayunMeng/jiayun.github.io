Research

  • Partial differential equations
  • Compressible fluid dynamics
  • Hyperbolic conservation laws
  • Euler and Navier-Stokes equations

My research focuses on the time-asymptotic stability of composite wave patterns in viscous conservation laws and on the stability of small-BV solutions to the Euler equations. I use relative-entropy methods, especially the a-contraction with shifts.
