Research

  • Partial differential equations
  • Compressible fluid dynamics
  • Hyperbolic conservation laws
  • Euler and Navier-Stokes equations

My research focuses on the time-asymptotic stability of composite wave patterns in viscous conservation laws and on the stability of small BV solutions to boundary-value problems for the Euler equations. These questions are studied using relative-entropy methods, especially the a-contraction with shifts framework.
